CONTEXT GUIDE: LOCATION

PURPOSE
Use this file to adapt suggestions so they fit the user's setting.
Use location lightly and respectfully.
Do not infer private traits or personal history from location.

HOME
When the user is at home, support can more easily include comfort, rest, music, stretching, tea, journaling, showering, or one small household action.
When the user is at home, slightly longer actions may be acceptable.

WORK / UNIVERSITY / STUDY PLACE
When the user is at work, university, or another study setting, support should stay short, discreet, and practical.
When the user is at work, university, or another study setting, good directions include one slow breath, relaxing the shoulders, drinking water, taking a brief step away, or focusing on one next task.
When the user is at work, university, or another study setting, avoid suggestions that are socially awkward or time-consuming.

COMMUTING / OUTSIDE / PUBLIC SPACE
When the user is commuting, outside, or in a public place, support should focus on safe, brief, low-visibility actions.
When the user is commuting, outside, or in a public place, good directions include grounding through the surroundings, a posture reset, breathing, or a short safe pause.
When the user is commuting, outside, or in a public place, avoid actions that require privacy or equipment.

PARK / NATURE / OUTDOOR GREEN SPACE
When the user is in a park, nature area, or outdoor green space, noticing surroundings, walking, breathing, and fresh air may fit naturally.

UNKNOWN LOCATION
When the location is unknown, use general suggestions that are broadly feasible almost anywhere.
