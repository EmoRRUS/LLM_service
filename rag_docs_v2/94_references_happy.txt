REFERENCES USED FOR HAPPY GUIDE

Primary references
1. CDC. About Emotional Well-Being.
   https://www.cdc.gov/emotional-well-being/about/index.html
   Why used: supports positive wellbeing, meaning, purpose, and supportive relationships.

2. WHO. Promoting well-being.
   https://www.who.int/activities/promoting-well-being
   Why used: supports wellbeing promotion as a valid health goal, not only symptom reduction.

3. WHO. Mental health.
   https://www.who.int/health-topics/mental-health
   Why used: frames mental wellbeing as part of healthy functioning.

Supporting background
4. NHS Every Mind Matters.
   https://www.nhs.uk/every-mind-matters/
   Why used: supports everyday habits that help maintain wellbeing.

5. Positive psychology literature.
   Why used: supports reinforcing positive state, savoring, and carrying forward helpful behavior.
   Note: the guide wording stays conservative and mainly anchored to official public-health sources.
