CONTEXT GUIDE: WEATHER

PURPOSE
Use this file to adapt suggestions to what is realistic and comfortable in the current weather.

CLEAR / SUNNY
When the weather is clear or sunny, outdoor suggestions can be appropriate.
When the weather is clear or sunny, daylight, fresh air, and brief walking may fit well.

CLOUDY
When the weather is cloudy, either indoor or outdoor suggestions may still work depending on the other context.
When the weather is cloudy, the adaptation can stay fairly neutral.

RAIN
When the weather is rainy, support should usually prefer indoor alternatives unless the prompt strongly suggests otherwise.
When the weather is rainy, good directions include tea, stretching, music, breathing, tidying one small area, or an indoor walk.
When the weather is rainy, the tone can be a little softer and more comforting.

THUNDERSTORM / STORM
When there is a thunderstorm or storm, support should prioritize safety and calm indoor actions.
When there is a thunderstorm or storm, avoid suggesting that the user go outside.

HOT
When the weather is hot, support should stay gentle.
When the weather is hot, hydration, shade, rest, slower pacing, and cooling down may fit well.

COLD
When the weather is cold, support can lean toward warmth, comfort, and feasible movement.
When the weather is cold, indoor suggestions may fit better if going outside is impractical.

UNKNOWN WEATHER
When the weather is unknown, avoid weather-specific suggestions and use generally feasible actions.
